# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** February 5, 2026  
**Organization:** Tingly Dev  
**Product:** Tingly Box  

Thank you for using **Tingly Box** (the “Product”). At Tingly Dev (“we,” “us,” or “our”), we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains clearly and transparently how we handle your personal information when you use Tingly Box.

## 1. We Do Not Collect Any Personal Data

Tingly Box is designed with user privacy as a core principle. **We do not collect, store, transmit, or process any of your personal data in any form**, including but not limited to:

- Name, email address, phone number  
- IP address, device identifiers (e.g., IDFA, Android ID)  
- Location data, browsing history, or usage behavior  
- Any information that could be used to identify you personally  

The Product operates entirely on your local device. Any data generated or used by the application remains solely on your device and is never sent to our servers or any third parties.

## 2. Third-Party Services

Tingly Box **does not integrate any third-party analytics tools, advertising SDKs, cloud services, or tracking technologies** (such as Google Analytics, Firebase, Facebook SDK, etc.). As a result, no third party can access your information through this Product.

## 3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Because Tingly Box is a locally-run application (please adjust if web-based), **we do not use cookies, web beacons, pixel tags, or any other tracking technologies**.

## 4. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us using the details below, and we will promptly delete it.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Although we currently collect no personal data, should our product functionality change significantly in the future (e.g., introducing features that require data collection), we will:
- Update this Privacy Policy in advance;
- Notify users via in-app messaging or other reasonable means;
- **Only begin collecting personal information after obtaining your explicit consent.**
